The Venue

Stadshaven Brouwerij

Located in the heart of Rotterdam's industrial district, Stadshaven Brouwerij is the perfect backdrop for our inaugural festival. This working brewery combines raw industrial aesthetics with the warmth of craft beer culture.

The venue's expansive brewing hall provides an authentic atmosphere where the sounds of electronic music blend naturally with the brewing process. From the fermentation tanks to the tasting room, every corner tells a story of craftsmanship and passion.

About Beats & Brews

Where passion for music meets love for craft beer

Beats & Brews Festival was born from a simple idea: bring together Rotterdam's vibrant music scene with its incredible craft beer culture. Founded by Shweta Panicker, a music lover and international beer judge, this festival celebrates the connections between sound, flavor, and community.

Our first edition promises an intimate experience where you can discover new artists, taste exceptional local beers, and connect with fellow music and beer lovers. From house and techno to disco and electronica, our lineup represents the diversity and creativity of Rotterdam's underground scene.
