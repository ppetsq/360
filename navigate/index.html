Meet Captain Ricardo

Real pilot writing real stories for young dreamers

Captain Ricardo Aguilar brings 31 years of aviation experience to children's literature. After graduating from the US Air Force Academy and serving 21 years in the Air Force, he continued his flying career with Southwest Airlines for 10 years.

Combining his love of poetry with his aviation expertise, Ricardo creates engaging, rhyming stories that inspire young readers to dream big. Through the Southwest Airlines Adopt-A-Pilot program, he volunteers at local schools, sharing his passion for both flying and reading.

His mission is simple: to write children's books about various professions that are both educational and fun, helping young people develop a love of reading while exploring what they might become someday.
